Believe it or not, your driving habits can significantly impact the experiences of those sharing the road with you. Aggressive lane changes may seem like a shortcut to some, but they often result in wasted time for the drivers trailing behind. Moreover, excessive braking caused by tailgating can lead to a startling 40% decrease in fuel efficiency, and we all know how expensive that can be. With this simulation, you can visualize how small choices can substantially impact traffic and environmental efficiency. It's a reminder that our individual driving decisions can collectively shape the overall efficiency and safety of our shared transportation network.
